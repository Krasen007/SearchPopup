## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"Search Popup transforms how you interact with text online. Select anything on any webpage and instantly get smart actions: search, copy, convert units/currencies, visit URLs, and more. Built for speed, privacy, and productivity."

## Value Proposition
**Stop switching tabs!** Search Popup brings all the actions you need directly to your text selection. Convert currencies while shopping, translate units while studying, search terms while researching - all without leaving your current page.
